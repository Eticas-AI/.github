Eticas.ai is a global company that evaluates and monitors AI systems in production, auditing not just the model but the full system: data flows, business rules, human oversight, and real-world outcomes.

Founded in 2012 by Dr. Gemma Galdon-Clavell, we've spent over a decade working with private and public sector organizations across the EU and the US to turn AI accountability from a stated principle into verifiable evidence — grounded in real-world testing rather than benchmarks alone.

We evaluate:

- **Expert & Predictive systems**
- **LLM-based systems**
- **Agentic AI**

...focusing on the failure modes that most affect real-world deployments.

## Open source

**Eticas AI Risk Taxonomy** *(repository launching soon)* — will track the ongoing evolution of the open framework behind our methodology, introduced in [*The Eticas AI Risk Taxonomy: Open Infrastructure for Operationalizing AI Audits*](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.02201).
